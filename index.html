About

I am a social scientist studying politics, inequality, and digital platforms. I am particularly interested in how AI evaluates deservingness and how cultural divisions become sources of political conflict.

I co-authored GoFailMe: The Unfulfilled Promise of Digital Crowdfunding (Stanford University Press). I received my Ph.D. in sociology from the University of Toronto.
